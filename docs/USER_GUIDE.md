# Travel Roots - User Guide

Welcome to **Travel Roots**, your family's centralized travel companion. This guide will help you navigate the platform, manage your trips, and make the most of our automated features.

---

## 1. Getting Started

Before adding your first trip, we recommend setting up your profile to ensure everything is personalized.

### Family Identity & Members
1.  Click the **Settings (Gear Icon)** in the top navigation bar.
2.  **Family Members**: Add everyone in your family who travels.
    *   Enter their **Name** and any **Nicknames** (this helps our AI identify them in emails!).
    *   Assign a **Role** (e.g., Traveler).
    *   *Tip: Adding accurate nicknames helps us automatically assign travelers to trips when you forward emails.*

### Linked Emails (Automated Imports)
The most powerful feature of Travel Roots is the ability to email your itineraries directly to your dashboard.
1.  In **Settings**, go to the **Linked Emails** tab.
2.  Add the personal email addresses you use to book travel.
3.  **Action**: Once linked, simply forward any flight, hotel, or activity confirmation email to:
    `mytravelroot@gmail.com`

---

## 2. Adding Trips

There are three ways to get trips into your dashboard:

### Method A: Email Forwarding (Recommended)
Forward your confirmation emails (PDFs or standard HTML emails) from your linked email address to `mytravelroot@gmail.com`. Our AI will parse the details and creates the trip for you automatically.

### Method B: File Upload
1.  Click **Trip Actions (+)** in the top right of the dashboard.
2.  Select **Import PDF / EML**.
3.  Drag and drop your itinerary file. We support PDF and .eml files.

### Method C: Manual Entry
1.  Click **Trip Actions (+)** > **Add Trip Manually**.
2.  Enter the **Location** and **Dates**.
3.  (Optional) Add an Activity Name (e.g., "Visiting Grandma").
4.  Select the **Travelers** going on this trip.

---

## 3. Your Dashboard

The dashboard is your central hub. It is designed to be beautiful, functional, and "glanceable."

### Views
*   **Upcoming**: Your future plans, sorted by nearest date.
*   **Completed**: A history of your past adventures.
*   **Map**: An interactive world map showing all your trip destinations.
*   **Cancelled / Credit**: An archive of cancelled trips, including a tracker for unused **Flight Credits**.

### Filtering & Search
*   **Traveler Filter**: Tap a family member's name (e.g., "Dad", "Mom") at the top of the list. The dashboard will update to show *only* the trips that person is on.
*   **Trip Groups**: Combine related trips into a **Group** (e.g., "Summer Europe Trip") to keep your list organized. You can create a group by selecting multiple trips in the "Trip Actions" menu.

---

## 4. Trip Details & Management

Click on any trip card to view its full itinerary.

### Itinerary Features
*   **Flights**: View airline, flight numbers, and times.
    *   **Copy Code**: Click any confirmation code to instantly copy it to your clipboard.
    *   **Check-In**: Use the "Check In Online" button for direct access to the airline's site.
*   **Accommodation**: Hotel names, addresses, and dates.
*   **Source Docs**: Click **View Source Docs** in the menu to see the original file you uploaded (perfect for double-checking details).

### Managing Travelers
*   **Add Traveler**: Click the **(+)** button in the travelers section to add a family member who decided to join later.
*   **Remove Traveler**: Hover over a traveler's avatar (or tap on mobile) to remove them from the trip.

### Credits & Cancellations
If a trip is cancelled, don't just delete it!
1.  Select **Cancel Trip / Credit** from the menu.
2.  Enter the **Flight Credit Amount**.
3.  You can apply the credit evenly across all passengers or assign specific amounts to individuals.
4.  (Optional) Add an expiration date to get reminders.
5.  The trip moves to the "Cancelled / Credit" tab, helping you ensure you never lose money on unused vouchers.

---

## 5. Sharing

Share your travel plans with friends or extended family easily.

### Public Links
1.  Open any Trip or Trip Group.
2.  Click **Share Trip** (or the Share icon).
3.  Toggle **Public Access** to **ON**.
4.  Copy the secure link.
    *   *Note: Public links are read-only. Viewers cannot see sensitive data like source documents or make changes.*
